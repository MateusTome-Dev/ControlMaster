Tutorial

1- After logging in (if necessary), look for an option in the menu or click on this option to access the product registration page. On the product registration page, you will find fields to fill in the product details.
2- Fill in the mandatory fields such as product name, description, available quantity, price, category, etc.
3- If there are additional options such as product images or advanced details, fill them in as necessary.
4- After filling in all the product details, review the information to ensure it is correct and complete.
5- Check for spelling errors, missing information, or inconsistencies in the provided data.
6- After reviewing the information, find the "Submit", "Save", or "Register" button on the page.
7- Click on this button to submit the product registration.
